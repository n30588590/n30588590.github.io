Manitoba Council for Exceptional Children Scholarships

Description

Four recipients are awarded $750.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-01-09T00:00:00.

Applications include a form.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in a field which relates to helping exceptional youths.

Applicants may otherwise be a student with exceptional needs enrolling in post-secondary study. These students must demonstrate a "Yes, I Can" attitude.
