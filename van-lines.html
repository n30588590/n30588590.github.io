North American Van Lines Logistics Scholarship Competition

Description

Two recipients are awarded $1000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Applications include a form, verification of enrolment, a transcript, and a 400-to-600-word essay detailing why logistics/supply chain management is important within their major of choice.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in the field of logistics or some related field.

Applicants and their immediate families must not be employed by or professionally affiliated with North American Van Lines.
