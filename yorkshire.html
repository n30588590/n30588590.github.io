Yorkshire Valley Farms Canadian Eco-Scholar Award

Description

Four recipients are awarded between $1000 and $5000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-02-09T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and either a 300-word essay or two-minute video describing how they are engaged in organics, how they intend to use their studies, and their actions to progress organic regenerative practices in their community.
