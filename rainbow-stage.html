Rainbow Stage Scholarship

Description

Up to four recipients are awarded $750.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-05-23T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and a resume.

Applicants must apply to a category, choosing from "Performance" (singers, dancers, and actors), "Technical/Production" (set technicians, wardrobe and makeup designers, production, prop building), and "Musician" (instrumentalists, composers, lyricists).

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in a program related to their application.
