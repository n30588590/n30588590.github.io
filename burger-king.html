Burger King Foundation Scholarship

Description

There are multiple streams of entry to this scholarship. Applicants should read the eligibility rules here.

Recipients are chosen based on an equal weighting of academic record, participation in school and community activities and work experience, and financial need.

An unknown number of recipients are awarded between $1000 and $60 000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2023-12-15T00:00:00.

Applications include a form.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.
