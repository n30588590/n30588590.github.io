Leonard Foundation Scholarship

Description

Around 100 recipients are awarded $1000 to $1500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-03-31T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and detailed financial information.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

Applicants must demonstrate true financial need to continue in their studies (annual family income under $60 000).
