Got a Spine Scholarship

Description

One recipient is awarded $500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-05-02T00:00:00.

Applications include a form, a 1000-word essay answering the following prompt: "What are three common ways people develop back problems? What are three ways that you can ensure that you do not experience chronic back pain in the future? What are three methods chiropractors use when treating back pain?", and a 250-word essay answering the following prompt: 'Why do you feel that you desesrve this scholarship?".

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.
