Dakota Community Centre Scholarship

Description

Up to three recipients are awarded $500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-03-31T00:00:00.

Applications include a transcript, two reference letters, a detailed sports-involvement history, a copy of an acceptance letter, and documentation detailing the significance of the Dakota Community Centre as a sport participant or volunteer.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

Applicants must live within the catchment area of the Dakota Community Centre.
