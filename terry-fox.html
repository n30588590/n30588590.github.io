Terry Fox Humanitarian Award

Description

Recipients are typically dedicated to the humanitarian ideals of Terry Fox, have courage in overcoming obstacles, and possess definite marks of distinction and persistence in the pursuit of excellent in academic, athletic, and civic life. They improve human welfare, make a difference, and have a positive impace on the community and others.

One recipient is awarded $28 000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2023-12-02T07:59:00Z.

Applications include a form, three references, and a transcript.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

Applicants must be in good academic standing.

Applicants must be involved in voluntary and uncompensated humanitarian activities.
