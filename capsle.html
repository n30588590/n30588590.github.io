Canadian Association for the Practical Study of Law in Educaction Bursary

Description

One recipient is awarded $1000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this email by 2024-07-31T00:00:00.

Applications include a proof of enrolment, a 500-word essay responding to the following prompts: "Why are you studying education and/or law? Include thoughtful commentary on the nexus between education and law.", and a resume.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in the fields of education or law.
