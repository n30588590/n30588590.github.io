Leavitt Machinery Scholarship Program

Description

One recipient is awarded $500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-05-01T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and an essay answering the following prompt: "Describe

a situation you or somebody you know was involved in where better safety procedures could have prevented an injury. Explain in detail what safety procedures you believe should have been in place to prevent the injury and how they would have prevented the injury.".

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.
