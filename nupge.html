National Union of Public and General Employees Scholarships

Description

Bursaries are also available here for families with a member in MGEU.

Up to nine recipients are awarded $2500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-07-07T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and an essay.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

An applicant's parent or guardian must be a member of Component Unions of NUPGE.

Other factors of eligibility can be found for specific scholarships.
