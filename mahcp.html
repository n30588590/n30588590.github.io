Manitoba Association of Healthcare Professionals Scholarship Fund

Description

Twelve recipients are awarded $500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-07-29T21:00:00Z.

Applications include a form, a transcript, a recommendation letter, a resume, a 500-word essay, and a university acceptance letter.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

An applicant's parent or guardian must be a member of the MAHCP in good standing.
