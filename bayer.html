Bayer Crop Science Opportunity Scholarship

Description

Up to thirty-two recipients are awarded $3000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-06-08T17:00:00Z.

Applications include a form, letters of reference, a transcript, and a 300-to-500-word essay answering the following prompt: "Canadian farmers play an important role in feeding the world in a sustainable way; what role will you play?".

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in the field of agriculture, food science, or culinary arts.

Applicants must demonstrate leadership and a contribution to the field of agriculture through volunteer involvement and extracurricular activities in agriculture.
