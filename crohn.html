Crohn’s and Colitis Canada’s AbbVie IBD Scholarship Program

Description

One recipient is also awarded the Clinton Shard Memorial Scholarship alongside their award.

Fifteen recipients are awarded $5000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-05-15T00:00:00.

Applications include a form, a proof of diagnosis form, an applicant consent form, two reference letters, and a 500-word essay detailing how they strive for success, overcome their disease, and inspire others.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

Applicants must be diagnosed with Crohn's disease or ulcerative colitis.
