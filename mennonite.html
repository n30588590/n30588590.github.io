Winnipeg Mennonite School Alumni Scholarship

Description

One recipient is awarded $1000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website.

Applications include a form, a cover letter describing career aspirations and deservingness, a resume, a 500-to-600-word essay about how WMS prepared them for life's challenges and how they are preparing for future challenges, and a current photo.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

Applicants must have attended Winnipeg Mennonite School, having graduated eighth grade and attended at least three years there.
