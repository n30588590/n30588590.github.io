Luke Santi Memorial Award for Student Achievement

Description

This award comes with a tour of the Perimeter Institute.

One recipient is awarded $1000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-05-31T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and reference letters.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in the field of physics.

Applicants must maintain an average of 80%.

Applicants must demonstrate a passion for physics outside the normal academic environment and interest in a wide range of activites.
