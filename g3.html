G3 Grow Beyond Scholarship

Description

Up to six recipients are awarded $5000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-05-03T00:00:00.

Applications include a form and a 1-to-2-minute video describing the applicant's vision for the future of agriculture, answering the following prompts: "What is your chosen field of study and why does it interest you? What is one major challenge facing the world which you believe could be addressed through innovation in the field of agriculture? Describe this innovation and how your studies can achieve it.".

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.
