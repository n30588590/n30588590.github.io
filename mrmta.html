Manitoba Registered Music Teachers' Association Music Scholarship Series

Description

Up to twelve recipients are awarded $500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2023-11-02T02:00:00Z. Late applications must be submitted by 2023-11-16T06:00:00Z.

Applicants must participate in a recital for their chosen discipline(s).

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must be skilled in piano, vocal, or instrumental performance.

Applicants must be taking lessons from an MRMTA member.
