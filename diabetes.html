Diabetes Hope Scholarship Program

Description

An unknown number of recipients are awarded $2000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications by 2024-03-04T00:00:00.

Applications include a form, a transcript, a current photo ID, and a list of four references.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study at a Canadian institution.

Applicants must have received care for type-one diabetes for a minimum of one year by a Canadian pediatric healthcare team.
