Loran Award

Description

This prestigious award comes with a colossal monetary prize, mentorship opportunities, networking, work experience funding, and even a retreat.

An unknown number of recipients are awarded up to over $100 000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2023-10-12T00:00:00Z.

This scholarship is awarded based on a holistic investigation of the applicant's characteristics, especially leadership abilities.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

Applicants must maintain an average of 85%.

Applicants must demonstrate strength in integrity, courage, compassion, determination, personal autonomy, and especially leadership.
