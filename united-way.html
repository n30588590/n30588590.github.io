United Way Youth Leaders in Action Scholarship

Description

Four recipients are awarded $500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2024-04-05T00:00:00.

Applications include a form, a reference form from a supervisor at a United Way Winnipeg agency partner, and a transcript.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must live in Winnipeg.

Applicants must be a participant, volunteer, or staff member at a United Way Winnipeg-funded organisation.

Applicants must demonstrate academic potential, leadership, community involvement, and perhaps financial need.
