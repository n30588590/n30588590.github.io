Description

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this email by 2024-03-31T00:00:00.

Applications include a form, a copy of a treaty card, a resume, a transcript, and a one-page essay.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in Manitoba.

Applicants must be Indigenous.

Applicants must exhibit financial need.
