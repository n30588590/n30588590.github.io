Scholarship Program for the Children of RBC Employees

Description

More information can be found on the RBC intranet.

Up to fifty recipients are awarded $2500.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Applications include a transcript, a resume, letters of reference, and a 1-page essay about career aspirations.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study.

An applicant's parent or guardian must be employed by RBC.
