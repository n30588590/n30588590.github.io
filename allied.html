Allied Van Lines Scholarship

Description

Four recipients are awarded $1000.

Additional details may be found at this website.

Application Requirements

Submit applications to this website by 2023-12-15T00:00:00.

Applications include a form, a transcript, and a 400-to-800-word essay detailing why logistics/supply chain management is important within the major of choice.

Applicant Restrictions

Applicants must enrol in post-secondary study in the field of logistics.

Applicants must not be the immediate family member of an employee or professional affiliate of Allied Van Lines.
